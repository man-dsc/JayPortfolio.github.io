About Me

Early Life and School

Born and raised in Calgary, Alberta, Canada, I developed a passion for technology and problem-solving early on. I graduated with distinction from my undergraduate program, earning a 3.5/4.0 GPA. During my studies, I explored fascinating courses like Databases and Neuro-Fuzzy & Soft Computing, a machine learning course that introduced me to the power of data science. These courses not only provided me with foundational technical skills but also sparked a deep interest in applying technology to solve real-world problems. For my final project in machine learning, I combined computer vision and convolutional neural networks to create a weather-registration system that helped users prepare their clothing for the day based on predicted conditions.
