About Me

Passionate about turning data into actionable insights

Early Life and School

Born and raised in Calgary, Alberta, Canada, I developed a passion for technology and problem-solving early on. I graduated with distinction in Electrical Engineering (Computer Engineering minor), earning a 3.5/4.0 cGPA. During my studies, I explored fascinating courses like Databases and Neuro-Fuzzy & Soft Computing, a machine learning course that introduced me to the power of data science. These courses not only provided me with foundational technical skills but also sparked a deep interest in applying technology to solve real-world problems. For my final project in machine learning, I combined computer vision and convolutional neural networks to create a weather-registration system that helped users prepare their clothing for the day based on predicted conditions.

Work Experience

I have worked in the software industry, including roles in the oil and gas and tech sectors, where I honed my skills in software development. While these positions provided valuable experience, I wanted to move beyond these roles and explore opportunities that aligned more closely with my interests and goals, in data science and business intelligence. This shift led me on a journey of exploration—completing courses in finance, learning new languages, and even working in sales.
